# Digital Banking Consumer Products System

#### A Private web-app that helps the central branch of banks to distribute and monitor all types of electronic banking products in their sub-branches.

## Here are some of its features:

##### - Sub-branches must first update their inventory in the system before registering a new request:


https://github.com/rezaaebraahimi/Bank-Keshavarzi-Iran-English/assets/117990649/6e765d03-8409-4e3b-8139-02d69f6e4789



##### - Then they can send a request including the type of products and its amount to the central branch:



https://github.com/rezaaebraahimi/Bank-Keshavarzi-Iran-English/assets/117990649/9ec66947-ba14-4bb8-aed1-6076b1d8cc90



##### - The central branch dashboard contains many sub-sections, such as the list of sub-branches and their asset lists:



https://github.com/rezaaebraahimi/Bank-Keshavarzi-Iran-English/assets/117990649/2e200d4f-6242-4b65-a1dc-7e319c3c28a0


##### - One of the most important parts is the treasury of the central branch, which manages the main inventory of the central branch and its products are distributed among the sub-branches:



https://github.com/rezaaebraahimi/Bank-Keshavarzi-Iran-English/assets/117990649/52831d41-c323-4f96-98fc-b5fc60da1daa


##### - Also, the admin of the central branch can approve or reject the request of users and allocate the requested product to them:



https://github.com/rezaaebraahimi/Bank-Keshavarzi-Iran-English/assets/117990649/2e6a51b0-8f63-44da-b536-0191056636c4



##### - In the other part, the central branch can allocate a part of treasury products to them without any request from the sub-branches:



https://github.com/rezaaebraahimi/Bank-Keshavarzi-Iran-English/assets/117990649/f9696d0a-85d7-4831-9ad5-33ec2b2be764



##### - There are other features such as suspending branches and sending notifications to all sub-branches, etc.:



https://github.com/rezaaebraahimi/Bank-Keshavarzi-Iran-English/assets/117990649/3b11dfdb-5610-4be3-8da2-876dd267b2de
